Hi, I am Samuel B. Martins (Samuka), Ph.D.

I am an assistant professor, data scientist, researcher, and YouTube creator. I am an enthusiast about machine learning and data science to improve decision-making. I have a solid theoretical background and practical experience developing ML solutions for many areas, especially (medical) image applications.

My YouTube channel, xavecoding, is dedicated to producing courses and tutorials on ML and computer science (programming, data structures, web development, ...). The channel is aimed at Portuguese speakers.

My research interests are:
