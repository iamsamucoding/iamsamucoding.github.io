About

I am an assistant professor at the Federal Institute of São Paulo, IFSP (Brazil), data scientist, researcher, boardgamer, and broadcaster. I am the current coordinator of the graduate program in Data Science at IFSP.

I am an enthusiastic in data science and medical image analysis that truly believes computer-based analysis will change the future of healthcare. I am very happy to be working in this direction. My main motivation is improving clinical routine using computer aided diagnosis.

My research interests are:
  • medical and biological image analysis
  • machine learning
  • visual analytics
  • pattern recognition
  • computational vision
